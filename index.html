about me

A little bit about me

I'm a Computer Science student at the University of Caloocan City who enjoys turning "what if" ideas into things people can actually click, use, and understand. I got into tech out of curiosity, and somewhere along the way, I started caring just as much about how things feel as how they work.

I've worked with Java, C#, HTML/CSS, and Figma, and I use AI tools like GitHub Copilot and Cursor to move faster and think bigger. So far, I've built a student portal, a Point-of-Sale system, and an e-commerce site with an AI chatbot that helps make user interactions smoother and a bit more human.

These days, I'm focused on UI/UX and front-end development — making sure the things I build aren't just functional, but actually make sense (and maybe even feel nice to use). I'm open to internships and collaborations in software development, AI, and web tech.
